Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2025

At our Ankit HealthCare Center , your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, email, phone number, date of birth, etc.
  • Appointment Data: doctor assigned, appointment time, health concerns.
  • System Usage: browser type, IP address, time spent on site, etc.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and manage appointments.
  • To contact patients or doctors regarding appointments and health records.
  • To improve system performance and user experience.

3. Data Security

We implement strict security protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All sensitive data is securely stored and encrypted.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless legally required or with your explicit consent.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and improve service functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. For any such requests, please contact our support team.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. All changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, feel free to contact us.
